# 💖 Sponsoring Clypra

Thank you for considering sponsoring Clypra! Your support keeps the project **free and open-source forever** while funding full-time development on new features, performance improvements, and platform support.

## Why Sponsor?

Clypra is built and maintained in the open — no VC funding, no ad revenue, no paywalled core features. Sponsorship is the primary way to keep it that way.

## FAQ

**Can my company use Clypra commercially without sponsoring?**
Yes — Clypra is MIT licensed. Sponsorship is voluntary and appreciated, not required.

**Can we sponsor a specific feature?**
Yes! Reach out at [sponsor@clypra.com](mailto:sponsor@clypra.com) to discuss custom arrangements.

**What if our company needs an NDA or custom agreement?**
No problem — contact us and we'll sort it out.

**Will our logo appear in the desktop app?**
Gold/Enterprise sponsors can have their logo appear in the in-app About screen. Let's talk.
